Garlic Naan

Garlic Naan is a soft, chewy Indian flatbread infused with the bold flavor of garlic and typically finished with melted butter or ghee.

Its garlicky aroma and slightly charred texture make it an absolute favorite at Indian restaurants and home kitchens alike..

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Warm water (as needed)
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Chopped cilantro (optional)

Steps to follow

  1. Mix dry ingredients, then add yogurt and water to form dough
  2. Let it rest for 1 hour
  3. Roll into oval shapes, sprinkle garlic and cilantro
  4. Cook on a hot skillet or tandoor
  5. Brush with butter and serve hot
