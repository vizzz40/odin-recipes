Mango Lassi

Mango Lassi is a refreshing, creamy yogurt-based drink that's perfect for cooling down after a spicy meal

Originating from the Indian subcontinent, this sweet and tangy beverage combines ripe mangoes, thick yogurt, and a touch of cardamom. It’s not just a drink — it’s dessert in a glass

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ripe mango (fresh or canned, chopped)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (full-fat works best)
  • 1/2 cup milk (adjust for consistency)
  • 2–3 tbsp sugar or honey (to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp ground cardamom (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Mint leaves (for garnish)

Steps to follow

  1. In a blender, combine mango, yogurt, milk, and sugar/honey
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy
  3. Add cardamom if using, and blend again briefly
  4. Adjust sweetness or thickness by adding more sugar or milk
  5. Serve chilled in a glass with a few ice cubes
  6. Garnish with mint leaves or a pinch of cardamom on top
